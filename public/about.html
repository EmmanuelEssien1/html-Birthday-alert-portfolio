About Me


My name is Emmanuel, and my journey in technology has been shaped by a deep passion for solving problems, building systems, and creating solutions that empower people and organizations. Over the years, I have combined hands-on technical training, academic study, and practical development experience to grow into a versatile IT professional and Full Stack Developer.

Educational and professional journey

2015 – Earned a Certificate in Computer Maintenance and Repairs at Joscotech Works, where I developed strong skills in diagnosing, repairing, and maintaining computer hardware.

2017 – Completed a Certificate in CCTV Installation and Maintenance at Deen-Technology and Integrated Solution, Ikeja Lagos State, Nigeria. This gave me practical expertise in security systems and surveillance technology.

2017 – Achieved CCNA (Cisco Certified Network Associate) certification, strengthening my knowledge in networking, routing, switching, and network security.

2021 – Graduated with a B.Sc. in Computer Science from the University of Cross River State, Calabar, Nigeria. This provided me with a strong academic foundation in computing, algorithms, and software engineering.

2024 – Expanded my knowledge through online education on Udemy, earning certificates in Cybersecurity and Full Stack Development in 2026, equipping me with modern skills in secure system design and end-to-end web application development.

2026 – Completed a Master’s Degree in Information Technology at Alfa University College, Subang Jaya, Malaysia, further deepening my expertise in advanced IT concepts, research, and innovation.

My Career Concern & Vision

My concern in improving my career in tech is to remain adaptable and innovative in a rapidly evolving industry. I am committed to:

  • Continuous Learning: Staying updated with emerging technologies such as AI, cloud computing, and advanced cybersecurity.
  • Community Contribution: Using my skills to support educational and community initiatives, empowering others to embrace technology.
Global Impact: Leveraging my diverse background in hardware, networking, security, and software development to create solutions that address real-world challenges.

My Greatest Achievement in Summary

From repairing computers in 2015 to earning a Master’s in Information Technology in 2026, my journey reflects resilience, growth, and a passion for technology. As a Full Stack Developer, I see myself not just as a coder, but as a problem solver, innovator, and contributor to the global tech ecosystem.
